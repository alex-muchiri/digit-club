Giving back to community

We love a green healthy environment.

Our day spent at the Joseph Kang'ethe Rehabilitation Center started at 10 AM. Joseph Kang'ethe Boys Home is a shelter for orphaned and at risk boys mainly from the streets of Nairobi. The day started with a friendly and very enjoyable football match with the boys at the home. Afterwards we gathered together in the main hall where we had fun with the boys, followed by an enriching Bible study session. We were able to impart valuable life lessons from the Bible and our own lives, such as never giving up and the value of confidence. We had brought along a number of trees that we wanted to plant at the boys home. We planted a grove, a mini arboretum with the help from the boys. We also showed them how to take care of the young trees so they would grow and be of benefit to others at the home.
